Summary: Latitude and Weather Conditions

For this project...

The purpose was to capture the potential relationship between latitude and four weather condidtions. The weather conditions considered was maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed. Python script was used to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator.

All these factors can be accessed in the Plots dropdown above with a detailed analysis of the findings made on January 5th 2017. The Comparison tab shows the graphs of all four factors together and the Data tab also shows the data collected with the google API.

Below were the tools used to capture this information on Jupyter Notebook:

    OpenWeatherAPI

    Python Libraries

    • Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Requests, Time, Scipy.stats, Citypy
